Robustness Gym: a framework for model evaluation

Deep neural networks are great for many NLP tasks but are often not robust to adversarial data corruptions, distribution shifts, and other harmful data manipulations. This can lead to vulnerabilities, limited generalization, and unsafe deployments. We introduce Robustness Gym: an easy-to-use and extendable framework to concisely evaluate robustness and diagnose model vulnerabilities.
